  How your Lustig pipeline works

  Chain: .dat ‚Üí load_rawdata ‚Üí trajectory from XYZ calibration (1/mm) ‚Üí scale to
  Nyquist ‚Üí ACR_data.mat ‚Üí Python notebook (torchkbnufft) normalizes traj to
  [‚ąíŌÄ,ŌÄ] + computes pipe DCF ‚Üí ACR_test.mat ‚Üí MATLAB spiral3d_cs_3D_hoom.m: traj
  √∑2ŌÄ ‚Üí [‚ąí0.5,0.5], IRT NUFFT3D, init im_dc = FT'*(data.*w), then fnlCg 15
  outer √ó 15 inner. Regularizers: TVOP3D + L1 ‚ÄĒ but XFM = 1, so L1 acts on image
  pixels, not wavelet coefficients. Weights 0.01/0.01 = untouched Lustig demo
  defaults.

  Densities ‚ÄĒ three-framework mismatch

  Your kcomps came from torchkbnufft (Pipe-Menon), not from IRT (which does
  recon) and not from Faraz's iterative-KB MATLAB DCF. Two structural facts:

  1. Known bug (your own 05-22 note): pipe DCF computed with
  im_size=(256,64,256) ‚ÄĒ cardiac leftover. Recon grid is [90,90,90]. DCF was for
  wrong, anisotropic grid. Never fixed.
  2. Critical: DCF only enters the init. NUFFT3D(k, 1, ‚Ä¶) built with w=1; fnlCg
  minimizes unweighted ‚ÄĖFx‚ąíy‚ÄĖ¬≤. This is exactly our measured conditioning trap ‚ÄĒ
  unpreconditioned NCG on spiral operator (density spread ~1.8e6) barely moves
  per iteration. Final image ‚Čą init + small correction. So CS output quality is
  dominated by whatever DCF made im_dc.

  Why Steve's densities failed

  Structural, not tuning. Steve has no per-sample DCF ‚ÄĒ his "density" is knorm
  (recon.py:cudarecon): per-grid-cell Gaussian-kernel weight sums on MS¬≥ grid,
  applied as post-gridding division k /= knorm. Plugged into Lustig pipeline as
  kcomps, every convention breaks:

  - Domain: grid array (MS¬≥) vs per-sample vector (425,984)
  - Operation: post-accumulation division ‚Č† pre-weighting data ‚ÄĒ not equivalent
  ops
  - Kernel/units: Gaussian kdist0sq=0.2, grid-index traj, no oversampling ‚ÄĒ vs
  IRT KB, 2√ó oversampled, [‚ąí0.5,0.5] traj
  - Edges: knorm‚Üíeps floor at empty cells ‚Üí 1/knorm explodes at k-space rim
  - Ordering: Steve's modular kidx=(idx+idxoff)%nuniquesmp (one-rep traj tiled)
  vs your [npts√óleaves√óreps] flatten

  Bad densities ‚Üí bad init ‚Üí fnlCg can't recover (final ‚Čą init). Expected
  failure.
